ABOUT US

Our cafe is known as Lepaq Kitchen. Lepaq Kitchen is a partnership business and comprised of four individuals. All of them are expert and hold a significant role. It is a cafe with a western theme but has the atmosphere of Negeri Sembilan. The happiness of our customer is one of our top priorities. Since many of our customers might enjoy taking pictures, we have decided to build a modern aesthetic cafe with a contemporary design. The food and drink we sell in the cafe are made by the premium ingredients. There was a wide selection of western food,local drinks and coffee available at our cafe. Our low entry fee makes it possible for anyone to come and experience it themselves.
